Next steps

GANs didn't bring many applications until now. The main reason is that they are known to be very hard to train because optimizing the generator and the discriminator at the same time. However, recent breakthroughs in GANs estimation, and in particular for images generation enable to produce very realistic images of everything you want, as long as you have enough training data. A key feature is also that you don't need that much images in your training dataset - a few thousands are enough. We think that they can help in many fields - architecture, product and web design, ....
